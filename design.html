When fundraising for charity organizations like Save the Children and UNICEF on my Instagram crochet shop, I used simple geometries and bright color palettes reminiscent of childrens books.

I wanted my largely adolescent market to feel like they had the luxury of variety despite the specificity of handcrafted goods. Thus, I repeated shapes and color schemes in my graphics.
