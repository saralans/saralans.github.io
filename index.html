BabyBlues App

My Int2Transcription Biohackathon 2024 team and I developed a Post Partum Depression (PPD) software application within the public health track. The app targets the eighth of postpartum women experience PPD, a severe and long-lasting depression, and one of the most under-treated health conditions for women. The application will utilize natural language processing to address the scarcity of real-time human resources, alongside virtual screening, mental health tracking, and social network features.

Bruin Books & Bites

My UCLA Hack on the Hill (HOTH XI) team and I developed BruinBites & Books, a UCLA-centric app designed to bond students with similar literary and dining interests, enhancing their university experience through the intersection of books and food. The mobile app was developed on Flutter with the Dart programming language and a Firebase cloud database. BruinBites & Book won first place in the mobile development category of this hackathon by ACM Hacks.
